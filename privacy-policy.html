Privacy Policy for Village Focus

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Village Focus ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

TL;DR: Village Focus does not collect, store, or share any personal data with us or any third party. Everything stays on your device. No analytics, no ads, no servers.

What data does the App store?

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Focus session history (start time, duration, completed status)
  • Coin balance
  • Buildings you have placed in your village
  • Your settings (e.g. whether you have completed onboarding)

This data is stored in a private database on your device using Android's standard storage mechanisms. It is never transmitted off your device. We have no servers and no access to your data.

What data does the App NOT collect?

The App does not collect or transmit:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, advertising ID)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, files, or other device data
  • Crash reports or analytics
  • Usage statistics

Permissions the App requests

The App may request the following permission on Android:

  • No internet permission required. The App functions completely offline.

Third-party services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Your rights under GDPR

Since we do not collect or process any of your personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide, delete, or correct. All your App data lives on your device and is fully under your control.

You can delete all App data at any time:

  • In the App: open Settings and tap Reset all data
  • Or: uninstall the App from your device, which deletes all App data permanently

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at the same URL where this policy is hosted. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect when changes were made.
